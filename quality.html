Quality Management System

PT Karya Presisi Indonesia is certified under ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System, demonstrating our commitment to delivering consistent quality and continuous improvement in all aspects of our operations.

Our certification scope covers the manufacturing of precision components for automotive and heavy equipment industries. We undergo regular audits to ensure compliance with international quality standards.

Certification Details

  • Standard: ISO 9001:2015
  • Scope: Precision Component Manufacturing
  • First Certified: 2012
  • Audit Frequency: Annual Surveillance

Quality Control Process

Our comprehensive 4-stage quality control ensures every product meets exact specifications

01

Incoming Inspection

  • Material verification against specifications
  • Supplier quality assessment
  • Material certificate review
  • Documentation verification
02

In-Process Control

  • Continuous production monitoring
  • Dimensional inspection at key stages
  • Process parameter control
  • Real-time quality checks
03

Final Inspection

  • 100% product inspection
  • CMM dimensional measurement
  • Functional testing procedures
  • Visual inspection standards
04

Documentation

  • Test certificates generation
  • Detailed inspection reports
  • Complete material traceability
  • Customer approval records

Quality Standards & Commitment

Material Specifications

All materials are sourced from certified suppliers and strictly follow specified standards. Material certificates are verified and maintained for full traceability.

Statistical Process Control

We implement SPC techniques to monitor and control production processes, enabling early detection and prevention of potential quality issues.

Full Traceability System

Complete traceability from raw material to finished product, including production batch records, inspection data, and delivery documentation.

Customer Requirements

We work closely with customers to understand and meet their specific quality requirements, including custom testing and documentation needs.

Continuous Improvement

Quality excellence is not a destination but a continuous journey. We are committed to ongoing improvement through regular internal audits, employee training programs, systematic corrective action procedures, and fostering a culture of quality awareness throughout our organization.
