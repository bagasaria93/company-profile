Welcome to PT Karya Presisi Indonesia

PT Karya Presisi Indonesia is a leading precision component manufacturer specializing in automotive and heavy equipment parts. Established in 2010, we have been delivering excellence through advanced manufacturing technology, stringent quality control, and dedicated professional team.

With our ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system and state-of-the-art production facilities, we serve major automotive and heavy equipment manufacturers across Indonesia, ensuring precision, reliability, and on-time delivery for every project.
