Production Facilities

State-of-the-Art Manufacturing Equipment

Our Manufacturing Facility

Our modern 10,000 square meter facility in Cikarang Industrial Estate is equipped with advanced manufacturing technology and optimized for efficient production workflow. We implement the 5S system throughout our facility to maintain clean, organized, and safe operations.

From raw material receiving to finished product shipping, every step is carefully planned and monitored to ensure precision, quality, and on-time delivery.

01

Production Floor

Our production floor spans over 6,000 square meters, featuring a modern and organized layout designed for optimized workflow efficiency. The facility is equipped with advanced ventilation and lighting systems to ensure a comfortable and safe working environment.

  • Clean and organized layout following 5S principles
  • Optimized workflow for maximum productivity
  • Advanced ventilation and lighting systems
02

CNC Machining Center

Our machining center houses over 30 state-of-the-art CNC machines from leading manufacturers including Mazak, DMG Mori, and Fanuc. These machines deliver exceptional precision with tolerances up to ±0.01mm.

  • 30+ CNC machines (3-axis and 5-axis milling)
  • Precision turning lathes with live tooling
  • Brands: Mazak, DMG Mori, Fanuc
  • Tolerance accuracy: ±0.01mm
03

Quality Control Laboratory

Our dedicated quality control laboratory is equipped with advanced inspection equipment to ensure every product meets stringent quality standards. We conduct comprehensive testing at every stage of production.

  • CMM (Coordinate Measuring Machine)
  • Hardness testing equipment (Rockwell & Vickers)
  • Surface roughness tester
  • Optical and digital microscope
  • Complete dimensional inspection tools
04

Warehouse & Logistics

Our 5,000 square meter warehouse is managed with a modern Warehouse Management System (WMS) to ensure efficient inventory control and product traceability. Temperature and humidity are carefully controlled to protect materials and finished products.

  • 5,000 m² storage capacity
  • WMS (Warehouse Management System)
  • Temperature & humidity controlled environment
  • Efficient loading dock for fast delivery
