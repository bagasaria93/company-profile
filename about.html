About PT Karya Presisi Indonesia

Leading the way in precision manufacturing since 2010

Company Profile

PT Karya Presisi Indonesia was established in 2010 with a vision to become Indonesia's premier precision component manufacturer. What started as a small workshop with a handful of dedicated craftsmen has evolved into a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility employing over 150 skilled professionals.

Over the past 14 years, we have consistently invested in cutting-edge technology and human resource development, positioning ourselves as a trusted partner for major automotive and heavy equipment manufacturers across Indonesia. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has enabled us to grow steadily year after year.

Today, we operate a modern 10,000 square meter facility in Cikarang Industrial Estate, equipped with advanced CNC machinery from world-renowned brands like Mazak, DMG Mori, and Fanuc. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification reflects our dedication to maintaining the highest quality standards in every aspect of our operations.

We take pride in our ability to deliver precision components that meet the most demanding specifications, supporting Indonesia's manufacturing sector with reliable, high-quality products. Our continued growth is a testament to the trust our clients place in us and the excellence our team delivers every single day.

Our Vision

To become Indonesia's leading precision manufacturing company, recognized for superior quality and innovation in automotive and heavy equipment components, while contributing significantly to the growth and competitiveness of the national industrial sector.

Our Mission

  • Deliver high-quality precision components that consistently meet and exceed international standards and customer expectations
  • Continuously innovate in production technology and processes to enhance efficiency, precision, and product quality
  • Build long-term, mutually beneficial partnerships with clients based on trust, reliability, and exceptional service
  • Develop a competent and professional workforce through continuous training and career development programs
  • Maintain sustainable and safe operations that respect environmental regulations and ensure employee well-being
