Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

The minimum order quantity varies depending on the product type and complexity. For standard products, our typical MOQ is 100 pieces, while custom orders may require different minimums. Please contact our sales team for detailed quotations and MOQ information specific to your requirements.

Our standard lead time is 4-6 weeks for regular products from order confirmation to delivery. Custom orders may require additional time based on design complexity and technical requirements. Rush orders can be accommodated subject to production capacity. We provide detailed delivery schedules upon order confirmation.

Yes, we specialize in custom precision component manufacturing. We accept orders based on your technical drawings, specifications, and requirements. Our engineering team will review your designs and provide technical feedback and quotations. We can also assist with design optimization for manufacturability.

We are certified under ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System and ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management System. All our products come with Certificate of Conformance (CoC) and material test certificates. We can also provide additional documentation as required by customers, including PPAP documentation for automotive applications.

We accept bank transfer as our primary payment method. For new clients, our standard terms are 50% down payment upon order confirmation and 50% before delivery. For established clients with good payment history, we can negotiate flexible payment terms. All payments should be made to our company bank account, details will be provided in the quotation.
